== Description ==

DocuPress is a modern and versatile free WordPress theme designed for medical professionals, healthcare organizations, clinics, and healthcare blogs. It boasts a user-friendly interface, making navigation effortless for both medical professionals and patients. The theme is fully responsive and mobile-friendly, ensuring an optimal user experience on all devices. With a range of customization options, users can personalize the theme to match their medical brand's identity without any coding knowledge.It also offers doctors and staff profiles, showcasing their specialties and qualifications. The theme includes a health blog section to share informative articles and updates on the latest medical trends and news. Testimonials can be featured to build trust and confidence among website visitors.The theme is compatible with popular plugins, extending its functionality further. Social media integration allows users to expand their online presence and engage with a broader audience. Overall, DocuPress empowers medical professionals to establish a strong online presence, grow their practice, and provide valuable healthcare information to the community.

== Installation ==

Download the theme zip file from your account on our website.
Go to Appearance > Themes in your WordPress dashboard.
Click the "Add New" button at the top of the screen, then click "Upload Theme".
Choose the theme zip file and click "Install Now".
Once the theme is installed, click the "Activate" button to activate it.
